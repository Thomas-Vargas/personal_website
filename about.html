About me

My journey into Web Development began with an interest in coding after taking Harvard's CS50 class. Soon affter, I fell in love with the creativity involved in Web Development and , despite its quirkiness, JavaScript quickly became my favorite language. Since then, I have worked my way through FreeCodeCamp, TheOdinProject and Full Stack Open, where I began working on my back end skills. During this time I polished my HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React skills while building my back end with Node.js, Express and Mongo.db. I take pride in teaching myself, although I am always looking to those around me for inspiration and help. While away from the keyboard, I enjoy skateboarding, disc golf, russian films and exploring the Twin Cities with my girlfriend and dog.
